Posters

Our Campaign

This poster shows how teasing and discrimination can hurt a person who is different. On the left side, the character is judged and bullied, while on the right side, people accept and support each other. The bright colors and happy expressions represent equality, respect, and kindness. The poster promotes gender equality and reminds everyone that everyone deserves to be accepted and treated fairly.

This poster was inspired by queer people who experience discrimination because of who they are, who they love, and how they express themselves. It was deeply influenced by the stories I read stories of women who love women being hated, men who love men being judged harshly, and many others who are treated unfairly simply for existing.

This poster is inspired by a real story shared by a student who experienced discrimination during a school dance audition. The student was excluded because of gender identity, which caused emotional pain and feelings of rejection. This poster aims to promote awareness, respect, acceptance, and equal opportunities for everyone.

This poster vividly captures the stark reality of the gender pay gap. It features two individuals standing side-by-side, performing the exact same tasks with the same level of dedication and expertise. Despite their identical roles and contributions, there is a visible disparity in their compensation. This image serves as a powerful reminder that hard work does not always result in equal reward, and it challenges us to confront the systemic biases that continue to undervalue certain voices in the workplace.
